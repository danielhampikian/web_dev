GIMM 300

Course Description:

The dense saturation of the web and mobile devices throughout
our culture has changed the way that our minds work and the nature
of human existence in intricate and interesting ways.

In this class you will learn the ability to use existing structures in
the web and mobile devices to create interactive and dynamic apps
and websites while simultaneously reflecting on and exploring the
philosophical implications of this ability.
